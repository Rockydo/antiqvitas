# M5 Han Taixue academy

The engine-valid `confucian_academy` at Jingzhao/Chang'an is a technical proxy
for the imperial Taixue. It is deliberately an institutional building rather
than a claim that later examination-era academies existed in AD 1.

## Source

- *History of Humanities*, University of Chicago Press, ["The Humanities in China: History and Challenges"](https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/doi/full/10.1086/710276): the Taixue system of higher education was founded under Emperor Wu of Han; it notes about 3,000 students by the reign of Emperor Cheng and Wang Mang's 5 CE dormitory project.

`CHI-TAIXUE` in `special_buildings.csv` refers to this source.
