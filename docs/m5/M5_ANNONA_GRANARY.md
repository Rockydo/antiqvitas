# M5 Roma annona granary

The Roman `granary` building is seeded at Roma as the engine-valid proxy for
the Augustan annona's public grain-storage and distribution infrastructure. It
does not assert a specific surviving building at the exact map point; it models
the capital-wide supply institution at the city scale.

## Source

- Oxford Classical Dictionary, ["annona (grain)"](https://academic.oup.com/edited-volume/61673/chapter-abstract/548063756): the public grain flow that sustained Rome drew chiefly on Sicily, Sardinia, Africa, and, from Augustus, Egypt.

`OCD-ANNONA` in `special_buildings.csv` refers to this source.
