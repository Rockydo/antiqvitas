# Štip / Astibo direct AD 1 toponym correction

The automatic Tier-2 layer previously assigned **Stobi** to the installed Štip
field. Stobi is a distinct ancient city, so its proximity record cannot serve
as Štip's label. The Municipality of Štip's historical account identifies the
place as ancient **Astibo / Astibos / Astibus** and records a settlement notice
under Tiberius; the Tabula Peutingeriana research index independently records
the Astibos name in the Stobi--Pautalia route context.

The direct layer uses the concise **Astibo** map form. It is only a city-field
label: it does not reconstruct a civic boundary, route geometry, legal status,
population, ethnicity, language uniformity, or political control.

Sources: Municipality of Štip, [Economic Potential of Municipality of Štip,
brief history](https://stip.gov.mk/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/ECONOMIC-POTENTIAL-OF-MUNICIPALITY-OF-STIP.pdf);
[Tabula Peutingeriana Astibos index](https://www1.ku.de/ggf/ag/tabula_peutingeriana/trefferanzeige_en.php?id=902).
