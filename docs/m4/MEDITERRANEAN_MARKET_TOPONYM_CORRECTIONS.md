# Mediterranean market-toponym corrections

The automatic Tier-2 queue pairs a game field with the closest qualifying
Pleiades point, which is useful for broad coverage but can be wrong at dense
ancient urban landscapes. This focused pass corrects four visible M5 market
or civic anchors where the market ledger and a direct, AD 1-active Pleiades
settlement identify the city itself.

| Installed key | AD 1 form | Pleiades place | Reason for direct override |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `aleppo` | Beroia | 658409 | The city point is Beroia; the prior broad adapter selected nearby Chalcis ad Belum. |
| `damascus` | Damascus | 678106 | The city point is Damascus; the prior remote adapter selected Mezze. |
| `ravenna` | Ravenna | 393480 | The city point is Ravenna; the prior remote adapter selected Augusta. |
| `sidon` | Sidon | 678393 | The city point is Sidon; the prior remote adapter selected Kana. |

The display bindings are city-point names only. They make no claim about a
city polygon, jurisdiction, population, local-language uniformity, or the
scope of the corresponding M5 market proxy.
