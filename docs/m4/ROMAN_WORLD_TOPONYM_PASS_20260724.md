# Roman-world direct toponym pass

Twelve city fields now use their direct ancient forms rather than neighbouring
Tier-2 settlements: Anagnia, Abellinum, Iguvium, Tibur, Velitrae, Faventia,
Dertona, Horta, Augustobona, Turnacum, Kallipolis, and Carnium. The records are
name-only adapters; none asserts a municipal boundary, population, legal
status, linguistic uniformity, or political control.
