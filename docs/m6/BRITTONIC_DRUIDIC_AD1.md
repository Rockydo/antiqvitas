# Brittonic druidic-authority adapter

The M6 privilege `antq_brittonic_druidic_council` is assigned only to the
eight pre-conquest Brittonic polity profiles: the Catuvellauni, Trinovantes,
Iceni, Brigantes, Atrebates, Silures, Ordovices, and Dumnonii. The British
Museum records the classical evidence for druids as religious leaders in Iron
Age Britain (`BM-DRU` in the source ledger), while the master plan specifically
calls for a druidic-council privilege.

The installed `clergy_estate` is an engine proxy for religious specialists. It
does not claim a uniform constitutional council, known officeholders, or a
common institution throughout Britain, and it is not assigned to Caledonian or
Irish SoP profiles. The existing kin-based elder council remains separate.
