# Trinovantian court AD 1 correction

Oxford's CCI 89.0006 records a Dubnovellaunos coin dated 20 BCE-AD 10, naming
him as ruler and the Trinovantes as state. Its separate Cantii authority
metadata and a British Museum coin associated with Trinovantes/Catuvellauni
make the political geography uncertain, but both date ranges include the AD 1
campaign boundary (`CCI-DUB` in the source ledger).

Dubnovellaunos therefore replaces the Trinovantian random ruler as an explicitly
contested campaign-boundary record. The existing tribal government, elder
council, and bounded druidic-authority privilege remain unchanged. No birth or
death date, genealogy, accession day, fixed Cantii relationship, later
Catuvellaunian sequence, or reconstructed constitutional office is scripted.
