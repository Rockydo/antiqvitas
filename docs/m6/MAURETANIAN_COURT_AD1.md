# Mauretanian court AD 1 roster slice

Ptolemy of Mauretania is added to the existing Juba II and Cleopatra Selene
court. The *Oxford Classical Dictionary* identifies him as their son and as
king from AD 23 to 40 (`OCD-PTO` in the source ledger).

The source does not establish a formal AD 1 designation. He therefore remains
a court character: the M6 government keeps Juba as ruler, Cleopatra Selene as
the technical consort, and intentionally leaves the engine-heir field empty.
This preserves the later succession evidence without turning it into an
unsupported appointment at the campaign boundary.
