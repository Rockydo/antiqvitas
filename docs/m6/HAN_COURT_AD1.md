# Han court AD 1 roster slice

Kong Guang is represented as a senior court figure during Emperor Ping's
minority. The *Han Shu* Wang Mang biography identifies him as a senior court
official and its Yuan Shi first-year decree appoints him Grand Master after his
Grand Tutor service (`CTP-KG` in the source ledger).

The record deliberately stops there. EU5's M6 character schema has no
historically faithful field for the changing Han office sequence, so Kong Guang
receives no engine office, day-level appointment, ability score, estate, or
regency role. `antq_kong_clan` is a narrow engine personal-identity label at
the Chang'an court, not a reconstructed genealogy.

This court addition does not change the separately blocked minority-regency
runtime contract recorded in `BLOCKERS.md`.
