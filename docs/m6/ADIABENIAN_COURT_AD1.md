# Adiabenian court AD 1 roster slice

Michal Marciak's peer-reviewed numismatic study places Monobazus I Bazaios
most likely from c. 5 BCE to AD 30, while *Encyclopaedia Iranica* describes
Monobazus as Adiabenian ruler in the first decades of the Christian era. The
two sources support replacing the anonymous Adiabenian start ruler with a
qualified Monobazus I record (`CAM-ADI` and `IRAN-ADI` in the source ledger).

The chronology remains contested because its published endpoints are
qualified. The engine term therefore starts at the campaign boundary only. No
pre-campaign accession day, biography date, family graph, marriage, religious
conversion, subject treaty, or later succession is created. The existing
Parthian sub-kingdom adapter remains a technical government proxy; M9 owns the
actual diplomatic relationship.
