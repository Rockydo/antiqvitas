# Han Wang-clan court AD 1 roster correction

The Western Han imperial house remains the House of Liu. Grand Empress Dowager
Wang and Wang Mang instead use the separate `antq_wang_clan` engine label. The
label represents documented consort kin, not a claim that the Wang family was
part of the reigning Liu dynasty, nor a reconstructed full genealogy.

Wang Shun is added as a constrained court figure. The Chinese Text Project data
record points to *Han Shu* and describes him as a Wang-clan official appointed
General of Chariots and Cavalry when Emperor Ping acceded (`CTP-WSH` in the
source ledger). Its English prose is automatically generated, so the start
data makes no stronger claim: there is no scripted birth or death date,
ongoing command, ability score, estate, office mechanic, or regency role.

The existing Han government continues to use the locally evidenced minority
regency form. This correction does not claim to resolve its separate runtime
fallback, which remains documented in `BLOCKERS.md`.
