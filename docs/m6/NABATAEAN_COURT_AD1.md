# Nabataean court AD 1 roster slice

The British Museum records Huldu as the first wife of Aretas IV during the
first twenty-two years of his reign and catalogues coins naming both rulers in
AD 1-2 (`BM-HULD` in the source ledger). This directly crosses the campaign
boundary and makes Huldu the documented consort for the existing Aretas IV
start profile.

The `consort` field maps that attested marriage only. It is not a claim that
Huldu exercised a defined office, an equal constitutional role, a fixed
biography date, a natal dynasty, or a reconstructed Nabataean succession. Her
separate court-line label preserves that limit while keeping the named queen
visible in the AD 1 government profile.
