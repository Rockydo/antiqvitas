# M4 Sulawesi Austronesian atlas batch

Date: 2026-07-21

This twenty-sixth source-led batch maps only the installed Sulawesi area to the
existing Austronesian proxy, resolving 57 previously unmapped controlled
locations. The audited ledger now has 315 selectors, 11,804 locations, and 143
mapped cultures; the generated catalogue remains at 165 cultures.

The reviewed archaeology places Austronesian movement into Sulawesi well before
AD 1 and records a local Early Metal/Austronesian occupation through the
campaign boundary. It does not support later Bugis or Makassarese identities,
an islandwide political unit, or a uniform language map. The row is therefore a
contested regional proxy rather than a reconstructed identity boundary.
