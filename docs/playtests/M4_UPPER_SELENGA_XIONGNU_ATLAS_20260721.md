including the Orkhon-Tuul core, to the existing Xiongnu proxy, resolving 23
previously unmapped controlled locations. The audited ledger now has 321

the source describes it as multiethnic, multicultural, and polyglot. The row is
therefore contested: it does not assign one identity or language to every local
community, fix an imperial boundary, or project the later Northern/Southern
Xiongnu split into AD 1.
