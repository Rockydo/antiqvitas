Date: 2026-07-21

unassigned Bohemian Marcomannic selector, resolving 40 more controlled
locations. The audited ledger now has 298 selectors, 11,266 locations, and
134 mapped cultures. Frisian, Cheruscan, Chattic, Semnonic, Hermunduric,

The labels are contested regional proxies. They do not create a tribal-border
atlas, project later standards or formations backward, or overwrite the current
Venedi representation of Silesia without a comparably strong source route.

230,000.000-thousand target preserved. The enabled-mod `make smoke` passed
with zero new normalized error-log lines. The global M4 density target remains
