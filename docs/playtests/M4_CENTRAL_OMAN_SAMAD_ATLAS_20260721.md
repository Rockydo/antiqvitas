# M4 central-Oman Samad atlas batch

Date: 2026-07-21

The thirty-sixth source-led M4 atlas batch assigns the installed Nizwa province
to a new `antq_samad` culture. Its seven locations are Bahla, Ibra, Ibri, Izki,
Nizwa, Sinaw, and Yanqul. This is a contested central-Oman archaeological proxy,
not an assertion of a tribe, language, state, or uniform Omani identity.

Paul Yule's Samad-period study places that material culture after 300 BCE and
through the late pre-Islamic period. Robert Carter distinguishes the Hellenized
northern and central Gulf islands from the inland eastern-Arabian and Oman
peninsula context. The selector is therefore limited to Nizwa; it does not
extend to the islands, UAE, or the varied Omani coast.

The resulting ledger has 325 selectors, 12,018 resolved locations, 150 mapped
cultures, and a 171-culture M4 definition catalogue.
