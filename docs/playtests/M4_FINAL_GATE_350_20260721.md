## Driver evidence integrity

The first attempt also exposed a driver issue: a minimized EU5 window could be
excluded before restoration, and a different topmost desktop application could
cover the game during a post-click settle capture. `tools/gamedriver.py` now
keeps title-matched minimized windows eligible for restoration and re-foregrounds
EU5 before a capture. The unrelated post-input image is deliberately excluded
from this report.

## Resolution and accepted gate

The two initial failures exposed a material generated-start defect: every
current `ruler_term` began exactly at `1.1.1`, which the installed parser
rejects as a future term. The generator now retains those records for source
audit but renders the native current `ruler`/`heir` fields only. The driver
also preserves an already-foreground EU5 window instead of repeating a focus
operation that Windows can reject.

The repaired autonomous run reached the paused AD 1 country selector and
Observer Mode, then rendered both required map modes. Its evidence is:

- `docs/screens/20260721_boundary_term_fix/boundary_fix_post_load.png`

All screens show `08:00, 1 January, 1`; the observer view displays the Age of
Principate and the Culture (Location) and Religions (Location) menus identify
their live dominant-culture and dominant-religion contracts. The final

zero new normalized smoke lines.
