# M4 Hami-Turpan Oasis atlas batch

Date: 2026-07-21

The forty-third source-led M4 atlas batch assigns the exact installed Hami and
Turpan anchors to new `antq_hami_oasis` and `antq_turpan_oasis` cultures. These
are contested oasis proxies, not claims about languages, ethnicities, state
boundaries, or the wider eastern Tarim Basin.

The cited source lists Kumul (Hami) and Turfan among ancient northern Silk Road
oasis states. It does not establish either oasis's AD 1 language or ethnicity.
The selectors are consequently only campaign-boundary oasis anchors, and the
established Iranian naming adapters are technical rendering only.

The resulting ledger has 335 selectors, 12,037 resolved locations, 158 mapped
cultures, and a 179-culture M4 definition catalogue.
