# Religions

The M4 religion foundation follows Part II section 11. It declares the ancient
faith keys and broad religious groups needed by the AD 1 population pass while
leaving dated foundations, schisms, state adoptions, councils, and conversion
mechanics to M10.

The entries distinguish civic polytheisms, Jewish communities, Arsacid
Zoroastrianism, Buddhist traditions, Indian traditions, East Asian ritual
systems, steppe and regional folk traditions, and American/Oceanic traditions.
Every historical assertion is tied to P11 and its recorded source hierarchy in
the generator tables. `contested` entries are broad population-map scaffolds,
not claims of a uniform institutional religion.
