# M11 Crisis of the Third Century illustration

The retained source is `assets_queue/generated_sources/antq_third_century_crisis_source.png`;
the 1080x440 master is `assets_queue/generated/antq_third_century_crisis_1080x440.png`;
the game-facing BC7 DDS is
`main_menu/gfx/interface/illustrations/event/antq_third_century_crisis.dds`.

The text-free image is generic late-spring central-Italian foothill context: an
unoccupied natural stream, weathered limestone, muted grassland, scattered
umbrella pines and oaks, low rounded hills, and distant pale mountains. It
identifies no emperor, battle, breakaway, plague, polity, city, date, or
outcome. It is not a reconstruction of the Crisis of the Third Century,
imperial succession, or Aurelianic recovery.

`antq_m10_third.3005` link. The inspected 1080x440 BC7 texture passed
`make validate` and enabled-mod `make smoke` with zero new error-log lines.
