# M11 event-art contact sheet

`make art-review` renders `contact_sheet.html` and `contact_sheet.png` from
every retained `assets_queue/generated/antq_*_1080x440.png` master. The HTML
is the browsable review surface; the PNG is the compact visual record suitable
for inclusion in the M11 milestone report.

The current review contains eighty-three 1080x440 event masters. It was inspected for
omissions, clipped labels, incorrect aspect ratios, readable text, watermarks,
or obvious anachronistic material. Individual asset records retain the more
specific historical-scope findings and engine checks.

Age-illustration masters are intentionally excluded; their separate review and
fixed engine contract are recorded in `M11_AGE_ART.md`.

Sources: ANTIQVITAS master plan section 20; retained project masters.
