The retained source is `assets_queue/generated_sources/antq_sassanid_revolution_source.png`;
the 1080x440 master is `assets_queue/generated/antq_sassanid_revolution_1080x440.png`;

The text-free image is generic early-spring Fars highland context: an
unoccupied natural seasonal stream between weathered warm-limestone slopes,
sparse grasses, low shrubs, scattered trees, and rounded distant mountains. It
identifies no ruler, battle, dynastic transition, polity, city, palace, sacred
site, date, or outcome. It is not a reconstruction of Ardashir's revolt, the
Sassanid Revolution, or Sassanid state formation.
