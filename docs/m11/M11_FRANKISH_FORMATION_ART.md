the 1080x440 master is `assets_queue/generated/antq_frankish_formation_1080x440.png`;

The text-free image is generic early-autumn Lower-Rhine floodplain context: an
unoccupied natural side-channel, willow and alder stands, reeds, damp meadow,
pale gravel, low wooded rises, and low cloud. It identifies no Frankish group,
person, migration, polity, settlement, limes, date, or outcome. It is not a
reconstruction of Frankish formation.
