# M11 Severan Settlement illustration

The retained source is `assets_queue/generated_sources/antq_five_emperors_source.png`;
the 1080x440 master is `assets_queue/generated/antq_five_emperors_1080x440.png`;
the game-facing BC7 DDS is
`main_menu/gfx/interface/illustrations/event/antq_five_emperors.dds`.

The text-free image is generic late-autumn central-Italian lowland context: an
unoccupied natural stream, olive trees, weathered limestone, dry grass, and
distant Apennine hills. It identifies no person, emperor, civil war, Severan,
polity, city, road, building, date, or outcome. It is not a reconstruction of
the Year of the Five Emperors or of the Severan settlement.

`EVENT_IMAGES` in `tools/m10_second_century.py` owns the
`antq_m10_second.2017` link. The inspected 1080x440 BC7 texture passed
`make validate` and enabled-mod `make smoke` with zero new error-log lines.

Sources: ANTIQVITAS master plan sections 8.1, 9, and 20; local EU5
country-event image contract.
