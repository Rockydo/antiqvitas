unoccupied natural stream, pale karst stone, rough grass, scattered trees,
weathered ridges, and distant mountains. It identifies no emperor, court,
Tetrarchy, reform, polity, city, palace, date, or outcome. It is not a
reconstruction of Diocletian, the Dominate, or the Tetrarchy.
