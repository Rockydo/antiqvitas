# M11 Verus' Parthian War illustration

The retained source is `assets_queue/generated_sources/antq_verus_parthia_source.png`;
the 1080x440 master is `assets_queue/generated/antq_verus_parthia_1080x440.png`;
the game-facing BC7 DDS is
`main_menu/gfx/interface/illustrations/event/antq_verus_parthia.dds`.

The text-free image is generic Mesopotamian river-margin context: empty calm
water, natural reeds and tamarisk, alluvial bank, sparse scrub, and distant low
hills. It identifies no person, war, polity, city, route, date, or outcome. It
is not a reconstruction of the Verus–Parthian War or Ctesiphon's sack.

`EVENT_IMAGES` in `tools/m10_second_century.py` owns the
`antq_m10_second.2011` link. The inspected 1080x440 BC7 texture passed
`make validate` and enabled-mod `make smoke` with zero new error-log lines.

Sources: ANTIQVITAS master plan sections 8.2, 9, and 20; local EU5
country-event image contract.
