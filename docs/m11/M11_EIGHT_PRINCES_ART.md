# M11 War of the Eight Princes illustration

The retained source is `assets_queue/generated_sources/antq_eight_princes_source.png`;
the 1080x440 master is `assets_queue/generated/antq_eight_princes_1080x440.png`;
the game-facing BC7 DDS is
`main_menu/gfx/interface/illustrations/event/antq_eight_princes.dds`.

The text-free image is generic late-autumn north-Chinese loess-valley context:
an unoccupied natural watercourse, loess hills, reed margins, sparse willows,
pale eroded earth, and distant ridges. It identifies no prince, court, battle,
polity, city, palace, date, or outcome. It is not a reconstruction of the War
of the Eight Princes, northern collapse, Eastern Jin, or the Sixteen Kingdoms.

`EVENT_IMAGES` in `tools/m10_third_century.py` owns the
`antq_m10_third.3009` link. The inspected 1080x440 BC7 texture passed
`make validate` and enabled-mod `make smoke` with zero new error-log lines.

Sources: ANTIQVITAS master plan sections 8.3, 9, 17.3, and 20; local EU5
