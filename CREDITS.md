# Credits

## Project art

The ANTIQVITAS thumbnail, AD 1 Rome loading screen, and future original 2D
assets are generated for this project through OpenAI's built-in image-generation
pipeline, then inspected and converted locally. The AD 1 Rome master and
engine-sized derivative are preserved under `assets_queue/`; its scope and
review record are in `docs/m11/M11_LOADING_SCREEN_AD1.md`. The project reuses
Europa Universalis V assets only where permitted for a game mod and does not
redistribute audio.

## Data sources

Formal source credits for Pleiades, ORBIS, CHGIS, and the historical datasets
listed in the master plan will be expanded as each pipeline is introduced.
